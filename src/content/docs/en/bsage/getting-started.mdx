BSage is an ontology-based knowledge management system — an AI assistant that automatically categorizes and connects information.

## What BSage Does

- **Automatic knowledge organization**: Automatically classifies and tags incoming information
- **Knowledge Graph**: Visually explore relationships between pieces of knowledge
- **18 plugins**: Integrations with Telegram, Slack, Discord, Email, and more
- **Natural language search**: Semantic search to find related knowledge

## Quick Start

### 1. Create an Account

Sign in with your BSVibe account at [sage.bsvibe.dev](https://sage.bsvibe.dev).

![BSage Dashboard](/images/screenshots/bsage-dashboard.png)

### 2. Set Up Plugins

Enable the plugins you want on the **Plugin Manager** page:
1. Click **Enable** on the desired plugin
2. Enter the required credentials (e.g., Telegram Bot Token)
3. Save settings

### 3. Start Chatting

Start a conversation with BSage on the **Chat** page. Enter questions, notes, or ideas freely — BSage automatically classifies and stores them.

### 4. Explore Your Knowledge

Visually explore the relationships between your collected knowledge on the **Knowledge Graph** page.
